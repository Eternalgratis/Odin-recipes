Nigerian Jollof Rice

Description

Jollof rice is a staple meal in Africa especially Nigeria and this recipe will be showing the details on how to make a delicious Nigerian Jollof Rice.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of rice
  • 2 red bell peppers
  • 1 can of plum tomatoes/5 medium sized tomatoes
  • 3 chilli peppers
  • 2 paprika peppers
  • 2 scotch bonnet
  • 1 cup of turkey or chicken broth
  • 120g of tomato paste
  • 2 large onions
  • 150ml of cooking oil
  • 4 tablespoons of butter

Note: The chilli pepper is highly optional

Steps

  1. Place your peppers, tomatoes, and garlic on a baking pan, drizzle some cooking oil on it and roast in the oven for few minutes
  2. Transfer all your roasted ingredients into a blender, add ginger, onions and blend to smooth
  3. In a cooking pan, preheat your cooking oil, add chopped onions, tomat paste and fry for 15 minjutes
  4. Introduce your pepper mix, seasonings and spices. Stir and fry for 5 minutes
  5. Now, add your rice in the pepper mix and fry for 5 minutes
  6. Add your turkey or chicken broth which shouldn't be 1 centimeter above the level of the rice
  7. Cover the cooking pot with a foil and the lid to trap in the heat in order to avoid adding more liquid
  8. Cooking time depends on the type of rice you are using. Basmati cooks less than 30 minutes
  9. It is important that you allow the rice to burn for a little bit in order to achieve the special Nigerian Jollof flavor
  10. Stir, add finely sliced onions, tomatoes and butter for moisture and extra flavor. YUM!
